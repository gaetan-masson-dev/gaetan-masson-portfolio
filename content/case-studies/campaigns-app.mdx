## Overview

The Campaigns App project involved designing and integrating a completely new marketing automation product into an existing CRM platform used by thousands of businesses. The challenge was to create a powerful marketing tool while maintaining consistency with the established platform.

## The Challenge

Marketing teams needed sophisticated campaign management capabilities, but the existing CRM platform lacked native marketing automation features. Users were forced to rely on third-party integrations, creating friction and data silos.

Key challenges included:

- Integrating complex new functionality without overwhelming existing users
- Maintaining UI consistency with the established CRM design system
- Ensuring seamless data flow between CRM contacts and campaign management
- Balancing power-user features with ease of use for newcomers

## Research & Discovery

We conducted interviews with 20 marketing professionals across different company sizes to understand their current workflows and pain points. Key insights included:

- Marketing teams needed to segment contacts dynamically based on CRM data
- Campaign performance metrics needed to flow back into the CRM
- Users wanted to build campaigns visually rather than through configuration screens
- Integration with existing email and automation tools was critical

## Solution

We designed a campaign builder that integrated directly into the CRM interface, allowing users to create sophisticated marketing campaigns using familiar CRM concepts like contacts, deals, and custom fields.

### Campaign Builder

The core feature was a visual campaign builder that allowed users to create multi-step campaigns by dragging and connecting actions. This approach made complex automation accessible to non-technical users.

### Audience Segmentation

We leveraged existing CRM segmentation logic, allowing users to target campaigns using the same filters they used for contacts and deals. This reduced learning curve and ensured consistency across the platform.

### Performance Dashboard

Campaign metrics were surfaced both in a dedicated dashboard and inline within the CRM, providing context-aware insights exactly where users needed them.

## Results

The Campaigns App successfully integrated marketing automation into the CRM platform while maintaining the simplicity users expected. The visual campaign builder became one of the most-used features, and customer feedback highlighted the seamless integration as a key differentiator.

## Key Learnings

- **Leverage existing patterns**: Using familiar CRM concepts for new features reduced training time
- **Progressive disclosure**: Complex features became accessible by hiding advanced options until needed
- **Integration matters**: Seamless data flow between products created a unified experience that users valued highly
